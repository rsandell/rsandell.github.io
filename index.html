About

I am a software engineer with a focus on integration and development of software development tools, mostly Java related.

I started my tools career in the Java R&D department at Sony Ericsson (now Sony Mobile) int the team that developed and maintained the J2ME SDK. Got bitten by the JUnit bug and moved on to more quality and auto testing related tools.

Now I am the lead engineer for the CI system based on Jenkins used by the Software R&D Organisation at Sony Mobile.

I made my first contribution to the Jenkins Community in 2010 with the Gerrit Trigger Plugin and have since then considered that community my home.
